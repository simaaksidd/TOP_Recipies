Garlic Padano Pasta

Description

Garlic Padano Pasta is a delicious dinner or lunch option to impress friends, family, or significant others!

Ingredients

  • 500g of Pasta of your choice
  • 1 Tbsp of Unsalted Butter
  • 1 tsp Olive Oil
  • 6 Garlic Cloves
  • 1/3 Cup of Chicken or Vegetable Stock
  • 400ml of Thickened Cream
  • 120g Baby Spinach, Washed
  • 60g Grana Padano Cheese
  • 5g Flat Leaf Parsley or Basil
  • Seasoning of Your Choice

Steps

  1. First, place a large saucepan of water onto a stovetop over a high heat, heavily season the water with sea salt flakes, and bring it to a boil.
  2. Mince six garlic cloves or add six cloves worth of garlic paste
  3. Immediately serve these smoothies while perfectly cold.
  4. Grate 60 grams of Padano or Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  5. Roughly chop 5g of flat leaf parsely or basil
  6. Add 500g of your preferred pasta to the boiling water
  7. Place a large pan onto your stovetop over a high heat and add in 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter along with 1 teaspoon of olive oil which will help prevent the butter from burning
  8. Add in the minced garlic and mix around to prevent burning
  9. Pour 1/3 cup of stock into the glaze
  10. pour in your heavy cream once the stock starts to boil
  11. season with salt and pepper to taste
  12. once boiling, lower the heat for seven minutes or when the sauce is redued to your liking
  13. add 120g of baby spinach
  14. add your pasta and a small scoop of pasta water to the reduced sauce
  15. add in your freshy grated Padano or Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  16. mix and allow to simmer for a couple minutes
  17. Adjust seasoning to taste
  18. Add to a plate and garnish with basil and cheese
