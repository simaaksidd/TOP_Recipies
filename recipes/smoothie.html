The Caribbean Way Smoothie

Description

...

...

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Strawberries
  • Papaya nectar or mango/pineapple juice
  • Turbinado(cane) sugar
  • ice

Steps

  1. First, place the peeled banana, strawberries with stems removed, papaya nectar, and sugar in a blender. Cover and puree.
  2. Next, add the ice. Cover again and blend until smooth.
  3. Immediately serve these smoothies while perfectly cold.
